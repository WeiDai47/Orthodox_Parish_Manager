How This Works
  • Event Creation: When you create events or sacraments with a specific time, they will be interpreted in your selected timezone.
  • Google Calendar Sync: Events synced to your Google Calendar will use the timezone you selected here.
  • All-Day Events: All-day events are not affected by timezone settings.
  • Default: If you haven't set a timezone, the system defaults to Eastern Time (America/New_York).
